🔧 Technical Architecture

Input Features:

  • SPX - S&P 500 Index (Stock Market Performance)
  • USO - Oil Fund Price (Energy Sector Indicator)
  • SLV - Silver Trust Price (Precious Metals Correlation)
  • EUR/USD - Currency Exchange Rate (Economic Stability)

Model Specifications:

  • Algorithm: Decision Tree Regressor
  • Max Depth: 10 (prevents overfitting)
  • Min Samples Split: 5 (ensures robust splits)
  • Random State: 42 (reproducible results)
